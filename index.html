// Project: Personal Shopper Optimization

Project Overview

Spearheaded the performance analysis for a Verizon AI assistant designed to help retail employees identify optimal customer deals based on real-time data.

The Challenge

Identified Ghost Usage discrepancies where high tool adoption didn't correlate with sales growth due to "loophole" patterns in the AI triggers.

The Strategy

  • Technical Audit: Reverse-engineered SQL logic to isolate specific employee behaviors.
  • Field Research: Conducted on-site interviews to uncover cultural barriers to AI adoption.

Results & Impact

Accuracy Closed KPI tracking loopholes for transparent reporting.
Adoption Increased actual usage to ~80% through product refinements.
Revenue Validated increase in Premium Plan and Perk sales.

// Project: AI Sales Compliance & Insight Engine

Project Overview

Collaborated with the AI Insights team to develop a monitoring system for customer-employee interactions. Used an LLM to analyze call transcripts to bridge the gap between sales volume and quality assurance.

The Solution

  • Semantic Modeling: Developed a LookerML layer to unify disparate data tables, creating a single source of truth for compliance tracking.
  • LLM Accuracy Auditing: Refined LLM prompts in collaboration with the Product Manager. Conducted manual audits to ensure an 80%+ accuracy rate.
  • Actionable Insights: Created a reporting structure to identify high-risk interaction patterns for proactive training.

Impact

Transparency Shifted the team from manual spot-checks to automated, high-confidence auditing.
Integrity Built data infrastructure ensuring sales met verbal confirmation requirements.
Refinement Contributed to iterative AI improvement by documenting complex edge cases.

// Project: Automated Customer Transparency

Project Overview

Identified a critical opportunity to enhance customer trust by introducing an automated confirmation system. Designed a framework to ensure high-value transactions were explicitly validated by customers post-interaction.

The Strategy

  • Identifying the Gap: Analyzed compliance audit data to recognize where complex sales interactions required a second layer of clarity.
  • The Safety Net Concept: Designed an SMS-based workflow allowing customers to review new services in real-time and provide digital confirmation.
  • Stakeholder Management: Socialized the proposal across Legal, CX, and Product teams to align with regulatory requirements.

Results & Impact

Leadership Buy-In Formalized an Opportunity Canvas for Senior Leadership, resulting in a project greenlight.
Operational Efficiency Created the blueprint to automate high-touch QA processes and improve Net Promoter Scores.
Problem Solving Translated deep data analysis into a tangible product vision that protects company integrity.
